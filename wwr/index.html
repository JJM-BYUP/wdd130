Family fun:

Whether you want to gently glide down the river's gently lapping waves or whether you want to experience the high-octane adrenaline rush of churning rapids, we have trips and classes for the whole family. Starting with a simple float for children 4 years old and and older, there are many other options for both adults and children depending on their age and skill levels.

Solo travelers

If you happen to be traveling solo, we can hook you up with a multitude of choices. You can hop in with any of our regular tours or you could rent either a raft or a kayaks to float on your own. We could also arrange a private tour if you'd prefer to fish or shoot the rapids one on one. From small tours to boat or kayak rentals, we have all the options you'll need at whatever level you care to experience.

Large groups

Do you have a family reunion or corporate retreat you need to plan for? We've got just the trip for you! With a four-week notice, we can plan an unforgettable adventure that everyone will talk about for years. Just give us a phone call or send us an email and we'll get you set up!
