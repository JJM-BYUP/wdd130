Wyoming

Wyoming is a US state that encompasses areas of mountains, deserts, and plains. It is the least populated state in our country despite being the 10th largest state in the USA. However, there is lots of wildlife, snow, and long-distance driving. Sites to see include Yellowstone National Park, the Grand Tetons, (and in my town) the Intermittent Springs. Things to do include summer and winter outdoor recreational activities, site-seeing, and cultural events.
